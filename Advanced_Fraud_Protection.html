Advanced Fraud Protection 
with
Predictive Analytics

Overview:

The system was using a outdated fraud prevention system which was being exploited.  The challenge was to repace this outdated and 

complicated fraud system with state of the art Fraud detection and prevention system. Reducing the false positive rate and improving the credit line management.



Solution:


  • Delivered an updated, modern fraud and risk management solution with fully supervised machine learning based algorithms.
  • Provide a more accurate scoring model based on big data sources and advanced predictive analytics. Leverage big data for expanded analytics on both positive and negative factors history.
  • Used 3rd party API's to gather more information about the incoming traffic and to discern genuine traffic and study traffic patterns.


Benefits:

  • Reduction in false positives
  • Improved credit line management for high income consumers.
